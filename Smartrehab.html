Game Details

Smart Rehab is a pioneering medical research initiative aimed at revolutionizing lower limb rehabilitation through the innovative integration of an exoskeleton and the Oculus Quest 2 virtual reality headset

Technical responsible - Programmer

  • Took a leading role in the development of immersive minigames using Unreal Engine 4, employing a combination of C++ programming and Blueprint scripting to create engaging and therapeutic experiences tailored to rehabilitation needs.
  • Played a central role in crafting the project's foundation, encompassing VR integration, intuitive user interface design, seamless integration of the exoskeleton as a gamepad, and comprehensive gameplay and system programming.
