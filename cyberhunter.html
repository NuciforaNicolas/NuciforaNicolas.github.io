Game Details

Venture into the world of cyber hunter! Take on the role of the most famous intergalactic bounty hunter. Blast your way through the various menacing creatures using your laser cannon and using your powers to increase your strength and defeat the boss and get his bounty. Collect as many global coins as you can scatter throughout the city or by looting defeated enemies in order to use your powers or restore lost life. Explore the five levels available, defeat all enemies and overcome obstacles in this fast-paced platformer
